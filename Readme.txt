/!\ Important informations about how the addon works /!\

	Because EsoRP need to share profils of characters to works, a software, called "EsoRP Updater.exe" is in charge of uploading your characters' profil and downloading the database of other's characters.
In order to execute all the steps transparently for you, an installation program, called "Install EsoRP.exe" is at the root of the addon. This program will update automatically the EsoRP client and LUA's files.
Indeed, it is very important all the programs is up-to-date before launch (otherwise the addon will crash or, worst, will crash all the system by corrumpting the database).
This version of the addon is a pre-release, meaning that it MUST NOT be launched by a non-tester (aka one's who preferes to use the final version).

I want to thanks in advance all the people participating at this pre-release version. Without you, it would be a much more harder to develop this addon ;)

--- Installation instructions ---
I tried to make the installation as easier as I can. So, you just have to launch "Install EsoRP.exe".
IF you received an antivirus alerte, please ignore it (it's a false-positive of the heuristic engine). I will be grateful to you send me informations about alert on the discord server (at least, the name of the antivirus program,
to ask at that antivirus's developpers setting the addon in the white-list).

--- How to get help ? ---
It's very simple and fast ! Just go to the Discord server and report your problem. It will be solved as fast as I can ! ;)
